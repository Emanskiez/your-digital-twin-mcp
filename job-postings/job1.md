About Us

At TechFielders, we specialise in connecting skilled field resources with some of the country’s most exciting ICT projects. Our team supports major technology integrators and vendors with on-demand technical labour, from device configuration, patching, racking and stacking, installs to site audits, Wi-Fi deployments, and equipment rollouts.

We’re all about opportunity, flexibility and growth. Whether you’re a student looking to gain industry experience, a graduate seeking to build your resume, or an experienced ICT contractor wanting to pick up extra work, TechFielders is your platform to step into the action.
